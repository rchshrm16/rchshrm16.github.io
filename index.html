About

Combining the power of Data and Artificial Intelligence with Sustainability expertise

At Greenalytix we strive to generate purposeful and operable Sustainability Intelligence in a responsible way.

We combine the power of Data and Artificial Intelligence with deep domain expertise to solve complex ESG and sustainability problems.

  • How do you calculate Scope 3 emissions accurately and at pace with minimal effort?
  • How do you gain actionable insights on Human and Social issues in your value chain?
  • How do you quantify Biodiversity loss and create actionable intelligence to reverse it?

With emerging regulations such as CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive) and SFDR (Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation), accurate and trustworthy Sustainability Data, actionable Intelligence is now mission critical.

Solving purposeful challenges

Supplier Intelligence, Scope 3 Intelligence
Quantifying Social, Human capital issues and Governance risks in the supply chain can be complicated, given the lack of data. Calculating and reducing Scope 3 emissions is equally cumbersome.
AI techniques such as Natural Language Processing and Geospatial Intelligence enable you to collect supplier adverse media data, environmental incidents and conduct sentiment analysis of your value chain.
AI powered automations make the process of Scope 3 data collection easier. For example, Cognitive automations can extract energy consumption data values from energy bills (PDFs or similar other documents) to populate an Operational ESG database automatically.
Sustainable Finance Intelligence

Social Intelligence
Advanced AI techniques to evaluate socio-political impacts on large Capital Projects and Liability projects. Managing emerging Social issues is a key challenge for large corporations. Automated monitoring of Social Media data to derive intelligence about upcoming protests, petitions and fund raising activities against approved Capital projects is very impactful in managing stakeholder expectations.
Biodiversity Loss Intelligence
With TNFD around the corner, enterprises are preparing to take stock of Nature related impacts. The EU Nature Restoration Law will soon bring mandatory reporting and accountability for biodiversity loss. A strong data driven approach with responsible automations is going to be key to measure and drive performance to start restoring nature.

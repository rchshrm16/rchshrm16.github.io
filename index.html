Everything you can do with Greenalytix

Greenalytix combines the power of Data and Artificial Intelligence with deep domain expertise to solve complex ESG and sustainability problems by leveraging the Greenalytix IDEA (Intelligence, Data Exploration and Analysis) platform.

SUPPLIER ESG INTELLIGENCE
Using the AI powered automation can enable to collect supplier data on adverse media news, SWOT Analysis of Suppliers on ESG Parameters and Sentiment Analysis of suppliers’ stakeholders on social media on any supplier ESG initiative.
SCOPE 3 INTELLIGENCE
AI powered automations can help make this process smooth. For example Cognitive automations can extract energy consumption data values from energy bills (PDFs or similar other documents) to populate an Operational ESG database automatically.
SAFETY INTELLIGENCE

SOCIAL INTELLIGENCE
Automated monitoring of Social Media data to derive intelligence about upcoming protests, petitions and fund raising activities against approved Capital projects is very impactful in managing stakeholder expectations.

IDEA

IDEA Platform

The greenalytix IDEA (Intelligence, Data Exploration and Analysis) platform has three layers:

IDEA AQUIRE
helps you to bring relevant ESG Datasets from external third party datasets (such as Factset, Lexis Nexis, Sustainlytics..) as well as from internal enterprise datasets (CRM, ERP, EHS/ESG system, Sharepoint, Spreadsheets and more). We have trained algorithms to look for relevant ESG datapoints and collate them into a coherent dataset.
IDEA ANALYSE
leverages an advanced ESG Ontology which has been built by training Natural Language Models on more than 5000 ESG/Sustainability reports. The underlying ESG Data Model helps connect internal and external datasets in a coherent manner to drive advanced ESG Intelligence to support with both reporting and performance.
IDEA VISUALISE
is the reporting and visualisation layer to help with performance and regulatory reporting. This layer can sit on top of existing ESG/EHS systems to help with mandatory/voluntary reporting aligned to multiple reporting formats.
